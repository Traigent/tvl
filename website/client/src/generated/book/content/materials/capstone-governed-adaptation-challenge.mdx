## Challenge Brief

Choose an AI workflow that changes under real operational pressure: a support bot, retrieval pipeline, model router, evaluation harness, or agent workflow. Write the contract you would want before allowing automated tuning.

## Required Outputs

- One TVL module with at least three meaningful TVARs.
- At least two structural constraints and two `env.context.*` feasibility checks.
- One staging overlay and one production or hotfix overlay.
- A description of the manifest fields required for promotion.

## Evaluation Rubric

### Contract Quality

- Are the TVARs decision-relevant and typed correctly?
- Do the objectives match what the team actually cares about?
- Does the promotion policy define a believable shipping standard?

### Safety Quality

- Are invalid combinations blocked structurally?
- Are environment-scoped limits kept in the correct boundary?
- Does the overlay strategy narrow risk rather than widen it?

### Operational Quality

- Can another engineer reproduce the decision later?
- Is rollback planning explicit?
- Would the evidence bundle stand up in an audit?

## Submission Prompt

Write a one-page defense of your design answering:

- Why these knobs?
- Why these constraints?
- Why this promotion threshold?
- What would make you refuse to ship even after optimization found a winner?
