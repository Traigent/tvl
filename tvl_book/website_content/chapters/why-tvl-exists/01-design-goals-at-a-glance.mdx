- **Explicit tuned variables** make the changeable surface reviewable instead of leaving it buried in prompts or runtime code.
- **Clear rule boundaries** separate structural design rules from operational preconditions over `env.context.*`.
- **Reusable evaluation hooks** keep metric definitions attached to objectives instead of scattered across scripts.
- **Governed promotion** requires declared evidence before production changes roll out.
- **Deterministic exports** make generated files and downstream artifacts reproducible in CI.

<ConceptCallout title="Night-Before-Launch Scenario" tone="example">

The onboarding chatbot is hours away from orientation week. A new model release promises higher quality,
but gateway latency just doubled. With TVL you keep the guardrails, narrow or widen the approved surface,
and rerun the optimizer instead of making frantic code edits. The system explores safely and only recommends
promotion when the declared evidence is strong enough.
