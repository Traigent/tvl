Once teams can expose agent variables explicitly, the next question is comparative: which pattern is
the right one to use under the current workload, budget, and failure mode? This chapter gives a
compact way to read pattern pages and compare them as disciplined engineering options.

<ConceptCallout title="Reading Strategy" tone="hint">

- Start with the scaffold family.
- Then read the tuned variables and decision axes.
- Only then decide whether the pattern deserves budget on your study plane.

</ConceptCallout>
