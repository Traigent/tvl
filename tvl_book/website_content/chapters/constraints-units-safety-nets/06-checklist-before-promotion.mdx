Before promotion review, confirm the contract still respects the boundary between design rules,
operational assumptions, and measured evidence:

1. All structural rules pass `tvl-check-structural` and leave at least one valid configuration.
2. Structural formulas use TVL syntax (`=`, `and`, `or`, `not`) rather than another language’s syntax.
3. Operational preconditions (`constraints.derived`) only reference `env.context.*`, not TVARs or measured outcomes.
4. Objectives and `metric_ref` names still match the evaluator that will produce promotion evidence.
5. Unit tests cover both valid and intentionally invalid configurations.
