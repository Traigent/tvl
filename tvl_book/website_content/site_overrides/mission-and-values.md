# Mission and Values: Traigent Labs

## Mission

Enable governed, trustworthy adaptation for AI systems by making Tuned Variable Language (TVL) the center of
configuration, optimization, and deployment. We help teams ship adaptive agents without sacrificing safety,
traceability, or economics.

## Vision

Every production AI pipeline expresses its knobs in TVL, optimizes them through TVO, and validates data with
DVL—closing the loop between experimentation and reliable operations. Engineers and operators share a common
language for change, and promotion decisions are grounded in transparent evidence.

## Core Principles

1. **Governed Adaptation**  
   Configuration must evolve, but never silently. Promotion gates, audit trails, and deterministic exports are
   first-class citizens in every workflow.

2. **Type-Safe Configuration**  
   TVL enforces explicit types, units, and constraints so optimizers explore confidently and downstream systems
   remain predictable.

3. **Evidence Over Anecdote**  
   Decisions rely on repeatable evaluations, reproducible manifests, and clear metrics—not late-night hunches.

4. **Composable Tooling**  
   The `tvl_tools` plug-ins, Triagent pipelines, and DVL suites are modular. Teams can script, automate, and
   extend them without leaving the safety envelope.

5. **Human-in-the-Loop Operations**  
   Automation accelerates discovery; humans provide judgement. Promotion workflows keep reviewers informed and
   empowered to stop changes when context demands it.

6. **Open Knowledge Sharing**  
   Specs, manifests, and integration guides live in version control so every team—inside or outside Traigent—can
   learn from proven patterns.

7. **Bias Toward Experimentation**  
   We encourage rapid iteration through tutorial scripts, walkthroughs, and playground scenarios while maintaining
   guardrails that prevent regressions.

## How We Work

- **TVL Specs** capture the configuration space, objectives, constraints, and promotion policy.
- **Triagent + TVO** evaluate candidates, enforce gates, and materialize promotion manifests.
- **DVL** ensures that data quality keeps pace with configuration changes.
- **Tooling** (CLI, Streamlit control center, automation scripts) keeps the full loop transparent.

This textbook documents the patterns, assets, and operational rituals we rely on. We invite contributions from
engineers, data scientists, and operations teams who want to make adaptive AI dependable.
